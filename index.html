Research Interests

My research interests primarily lie in Large Language Model Optimization, Deep Learning Systems, and Efficient Model Inference. I am dedicated to addressing the performance and memory bottlenecks in AI model training and inference through algorithm-system co-design. I possess strong communication and teamwork skills, can independently analyze and solve problems, and have a keen interest in deep and challenging work.
